How We Work

Impressions Design Manufacture Finishing

Scanning

Our goal is to develop the highest quality digital image in our CAD system.

You can send us the digital files of your in-office scans directly to our system. We can deliver your order faster of the higherst quality.
We will provide you with a link so that you can track the progress of your order.
We also take the traditional route where you can also mail us your dental impressions we then pour stone casts and devlop CAD images in our state of the scanner.

Designing

Our technicians use several CAD systems and software packages to design and model the fit to your specifications.

We can provide you with a link to track the progress of your work or approve the final design before manufacturing.

Production

We use the Ivoclar Digital milling machines for the highest quality pieces

Our Technicians then manually finish the tooth to specification to provide a natural finish.
